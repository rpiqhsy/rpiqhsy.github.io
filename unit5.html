Unit 5

词汇

alternative

  • n. 可能的选择；选择对象

    1. have the alternative of doing sth. 有...的选择
    2. an alternative to sth. ...的代替品
    3. have no alternative but to do sth. 别无选择做某事
    4. There's no alternative ... ...别无选择

  • adj. 供选择的；其他的

    1. alternative energy 替代能源
    2. alternative lifestyle 另类生活方式

  • adv. alternativey

不得不做某事：

  • have no choice but to do sth
  • can't do sth bu do sth
  • can do nothing but do sth

interrupt

  • vt. 打断，暂时中断或阻止

    1. interrupt A with sth. 因...打断 A
    2. be interrupted by sth. 被...打断

  • n. interruption 打扰，插嘴

    1. without interruption 连续地，未中断地

assume

  • vt. 假定；设想

    1. assume sb as / to be ...
    2. assume that
    3. It is assumed that ...

  • n. assumption 假定；设想

    1. make an assumption (= assume)
    2. on the assumption that (= assuming that)

条件状语从句引导词：

  • suppose/supposing/providing/provided
  • if
  • on condition that

significance

  • n. 重要性；意义；意思

    1. be of (great/little/no) significance to （= be significant to）
    2. attach (great) significance/importance of

  • adj. significant 重要的；有意义的

    1. It is significant that...

of+n. = adj. :

  • of significance = significant
  • of importance = important
  • of value = valuable
  • of help = helpful

arrest

  • v. 逮捕；吸引

    1. arrest sb for some reason
    2. arrest/draw/catch one's attention/eyes
    3. sb get arrested

  • n. 逮捕，拘留

    1. be under arrest

介词短语表时态(under/in)

  • be under attack/
  • be in operation

短语

regardless of

  1. regardless of
  2. regardless of the fact that
  3. Despite/In spite of the fact that

cut

  • cut up 切碎
  • cut down 减少，砍倒
  • cut in 打断
  • cut out 切掉，删掉
  • cut away 切除，砍掉
  • cut off 中断（水，电）供应，使隔绝

feed

  • sb feed on sth
  • feed sb on sth
  • feed sth to sb
  • be/get fed up with

对...厌烦： be bored with/tired of be fed up with

date

  • date from/back to (+ 过去的时间点）
  • date back (+ 时间段)
  • up to date 最新的；到目前为止
  • out of date 过时的

句式

suggest

  1. suggest 表“表明，暗示”时从句不用虚拟
  2. suggest 表“建议”时，从句要虚拟(should)
  3. suggest 加动名词作宾语

as 定语从句

 

If only

  1. If only 要是...就好了 没有主句，引导从句用虚拟： If only sb had done sth 与过去事实相反 If only sb did sth 与现在事实相反 If only sb would/could/might do sth 与将来事实相反
  2. Only if ... 只要... 引导条件状语从句，位于句首，主句要部分倒装

to do 作结果状语

  1. to do 作结果状语表示意外结果，可加only强调
  2. 现在分词作结果状语，表示自然而然的结果，可加thus强调
